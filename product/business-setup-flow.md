# Business Setup Flow

## Target Flow

1. Investor provides intent, budget, location, business type, and preferences.
2. Platform returns suitable business options.
3. Investor selects or saves an option.
4. Platform builds setup plan:
   - property requirements;
   - documents;
   - partners;
   - providers;
   - equipment;
   - essentials;
   - monitoring;
   - cost estimate;
   - timeline.
5. Investor confirms plan.
6. Payment is initiated.
7. Setup milestones are tracked.
8. Launch readiness is marked.
9. Portfolio begins tracking expenses, earnings, assignments, and support.

## Current Gap

The backend has foundational business/profile models, but setup-plan-specific entities and routes do not exist yet.
