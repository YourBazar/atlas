# User Roles

## Investor

Wants to discover, fund, launch, and track local businesses.

Current implementation:

- Role route and landing page in Cygnus.
- Portal type in Centaurus.
- Business and business profile CRUD.

Planned direction:

- Business discovery.
- Draft setup plans.
- Property selection.
- Payments.
- Portfolio and earnings.
- Support.

## Partner

Contributes skills, operations, staffing, or execution support.

Current implementation:

- Role route and landing page in Cygnus.
- Portal type in Centaurus.
- Future model surface through `BusinessPartners`.

Planned direction:

- Skill profile.
- Verification.
- Assignment requests.
- Milestone updates.
- Payouts.

## Provider

Supplies property, equipment, services, monitoring, documentation, or other setup support.

Current implementation:

- Role route and landing page in Cygnus.
- Portal type in Centaurus.
- Future model surface through `ProviderPortals`.

Planned direction:

- Service catalog.
- Availability and pricing.
- Assignment requests.
- Proof upload.
- Payouts.

## Admin

Not implemented yet. Planned docs describe verification, setup monitoring, refunds, disputes, catalog management, and platform configuration.
