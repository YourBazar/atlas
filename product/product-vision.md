# Product Vision

YourBazar helps people launch and manage local businesses by connecting Investors, Partners, Providers, property, equipment, supplies, setup services, and ongoing operational support.

## Core Journey

1. User signs up.
2. User chooses or completes a role profile.
3. Investor explores local business options.
4. Investor selects location, budget, property, partners, and providers.
5. Platform produces a setup plan.
6. Investor confirms and pays.
7. Setup progresses through milestones.
8. Business becomes active.
9. Investor, Partner, and Provider track portfolio, earnings, expenses, support, and service renewals.

## Strategic Direction

The product is moving from role landing pages and CRUD foundations toward full setup orchestration.
