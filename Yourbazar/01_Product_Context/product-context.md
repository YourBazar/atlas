# Product Context

YourBazar connects Investors, Partners, Providers, properties, setup services, and ongoing support to launch and manage local businesses.

## Current MVP

- Signup and signin.
- Role route areas.
- Role portals.
- Businesses and business profiles.

## Target Product Direction

- Business option discovery.
- Setup planning.
- Property selection.
- Partner/provider assignment.
- Payments and refunds.
- Portfolio, earnings, expenses.
- Support and admin workflows.
